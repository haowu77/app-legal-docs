Introduction

BlingBling ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS/iPadOS/macOS/visionOS application ("App").

Data Collection

BlingBling does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside your device.

All audio processing and visualization occur entirely on your device using iOS public APIs:

  • AVAudioEngine - Real-time microphone audio capture for visualization
  • AVFoundation - Video recording of visualizations
  • Accelerate (vDSP) - Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) audio analysis
  • MultipeerConnectivity - Local network device synchronization (optional)
  • Core Haptics - Haptic feedback synchronized with audio
  • Photos - Saving recorded videos to your photo library
  • Local storage via UserDefaults (settings only)

Permissions Required

BlingBling requests the following permissions for core functionality:

Microphone Access

Purpose: Capture real-time audio input to generate visual effects synchronized with music or sound.

  • Audio is processed instantly for visualization
  • Audio is NOT recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Only frequency spectrum data is analyzed (via FFT)
  • You can revoke this permission anytime in Settings

Photo Library Access

Purpose: Save recorded visualization videos to your Photos app.

  • Only used when you explicitly tap the "Record" button
  • Videos are saved locally on your device
  • No photos or videos are accessed or uploaded

Local Network Access (Optional)

Purpose: Enable multi-device synchronization feature (up to 8 devices).

  • Only used if you enable "Multi-Device Sync" feature
  • Uses MultipeerConnectivity for local network discovery
  • No internet connection required - works on local WiFi only
  • Only audio spectrum data is shared between your devices
  • No data leaves your local network

Data Usage

All data processing happens locally on your device:

  • Audio Analysis: Real-time FFT processing (1024 samples, 64 frequency bands)
  • Visual Rendering: 18 visualization modes with particle systems
  • Video Recording: Recorded videos are saved to your Photos library
  • Settings Storage: Visual mode preferences, scene presets saved in UserDefaults
  • Device Sync: Audio spectrum synchronized across your devices (local network only)

No audio, video, or personal data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

BlingBling does not use any third-party:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection SDKs
  • Cloud storage services
  • Tracking technologies
  • Social media integrations

In-App Purchases

When you make an in-app purchase (subscription or one-time purchase) through the App Store:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple
  • We receive only anonymized transaction verification from StoreKit 2.0
  • We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or billing address
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device via StoreKit

Data Storage

BlingBling stores the following data locally on your device:

  • User Preferences: Visual mode, sensitivity, particle settings (UserDefaults)
  • Scene Presets: Custom scene configurations you create
  • Pro Status: In-app purchase verification (via StoreKit)
  • Recorded Videos: Saved to your Photos library (managed by iOS)

All data is encrypted at rest by iOS and is never synced to iCloud or external servers.

Multi-Device Sync

If you enable the optional Multi-Device Sync feature:

  • Uses MultipeerConnectivity for peer-to-peer local network communication
  • Only shares: audio spectrum data, current visual mode, scene changes
  • Data never leaves your local WiFi network
  • No internet connection required
  • Sync stops when you disable the feature or close the app

Children's Privacy

BlingBling is suitable for users of all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect any information from children, as we do not collect any information at all. All processing occurs on-device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access, modify, export, or delete from our servers
  • All data stored on your device can be deleted by:
    • Uninstalling the app (deletes all app data)
    • Resetting settings within the app
    • Manually deleting recorded videos from Photos app
