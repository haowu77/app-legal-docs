Introduction

MediaHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application ("App").

Data Collection

MediaHub does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside your device.

MediaHub is a local-first application. All your media asset management happens entirely on your device.

Device Permissions

MediaHub requests the following permissions to function:

Photo Library Access

  • We request access to your Photo Library to allow you to import photos and videos into your projects
  • We use PHPhotoLibrary to reference media assets without copying them
  • Your photos and videos are never uploaded to any server
  • We only access the specific items you choose to import

File Access

  • We request access to files you select from the Files app
  • We use Security-Scoped Bookmarks to maintain references to your files
  • Files are referenced, not copied, saving your storage space
  • We only access files you explicitly choose to import

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData:

  • Project data: Project names, creation dates, and settings
  • Asset references: References to your media files (not copies)
  • Thumbnails: Small preview images generated locally
  • User preferences: App settings stored in UserDefaults

All data is encrypted at rest by iOS and is never synced to external servers.

Reference Mode

MediaHub uses a "reference mode" approach: We store references to your files rather than copying them. This means:

  • Your original files remain in their original locations
  • No duplicate files are created
  • Minimal additional storage is used
  • Deleting a reference in MediaHub does NOT delete your original file

Third-Party Services

MediaHub does not use any third-party:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection SDKs
  • Cloud storage services
  • Tracking technologies

In-App Purchases

When you make an in-app purchase through the App Store:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple
  • We receive only anonymized transaction information
  • We do not have access to your payment details

Children's Privacy

MediaHub is suitable for users of all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect any information from children, as we do not collect any information at all.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All data stored on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Compliance

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
